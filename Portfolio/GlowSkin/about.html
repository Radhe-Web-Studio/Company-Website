OUR STORY

Born from a Passion for Healthy Skin

GlowSkin was founded in 2012 by Dr. Ananya Mehta with one belief: everyone deserves healthy, confident skin. What began as a boutique dermatology practice has grown into a full-service skin clinic with 15+ specialists serving over 10,000 clients.

We blend clinical precision with a warm, welcoming environment — because great skin care is as much about how you feel as how you look.

  • Founded by a board-certified dermatologist
  • Clinic renovated with latest tech in 2024
  • 100% cruelty-free & ethical treatments
  • Ongoing research & clinical training
