OUR STORY

Built on a Passion for Better Smiles

SmileCare was founded in 2009 by Dr. Priya Sharma with a single mission: to make high-quality dental care accessible, comfortable, and affordable for everyone. What started as a small two-chair practice has grown into a full-service clinic with a team of 20+ specialists.

Today, we combine the warmth of a family practice with the capabilities of a modern dental centre — serving over 5,000 patients across all age groups.

  • Founded by board-certified specialists
  • State-of-the-art facility, renovated in 2023
  • Patient-first approach in everything we do
  • Committed to ongoing education & training
