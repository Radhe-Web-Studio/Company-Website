FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a residential project usually take? +

Most residential projects take 8–14 months depending on size, design complexity, and finishes. We provide a detailed milestone schedule before construction begins.

Do you provide architectural design services? +

Yes — we offer end-to-end design including floor plans, 3D walkthroughs, structural designs, and MEP drawings. Design is included in our turnkey packages.

What is your payment schedule? +

Payments are milestone-linked — typically 10% advance, then staged payments tied to verified completion (foundation, structure, finishing, handover). No upfront full payment ever.

Do you offer any warranty? +

Yes — we provide a 5-year structural warranty and 1-year warranty on all finishing work. Detailed warranty documents are part of the handover package.

Which cities do you operate in? +

We're active in Mumbai, Pune, Nashik, Thane, Lonavala, Alibaug, Aurangabad, and Kolhapur. For projects in other cities, please reach out — we may be able to accommodate.
