Who We Are

We Build More Than Structures

Founded in 2001, BuildRight Constructions started as a small residential builder in Mumbai. Over two decades, we've grown into one of India's most respected full-service construction firms — completing 500+ projects across residential, commercial, and infrastructure sectors.

Our philosophy is simple: every project deserves the same level of dedication, craftsmanship, and transparency — whether it's a family home or a 20-storey tower.

  • ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management
  • 500+ projects delivered across 12 cities
  • Team of 200+ engineers, architects & specialists
  • Zero major safety incidents in 10+ years
